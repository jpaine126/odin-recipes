Pad Kra Pow Recipe

Description

This is a Pad Kra Pow recipe.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Ground Beef
  • Shallot
  • Garlic
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Saute the beef, shallot, and garlic in a wok
  3. Add the soy and fish sauce the the wok and reduce
  4. Serve over rice
